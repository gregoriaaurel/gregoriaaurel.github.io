Typoetry

Typoetry, a portmanteau word, is an exploration and expression of typeface by interpreting a poem.
The typeface is designed through transformation based on the moods and characters of the poem; Daffodils. All of the letters in the typeface are hand-drawn and manually colored using color pencil and watercolor.

Local Delights

Local Delights is a project that starts with a typewalk, which is to explore the realm of found typography in Singapore. The purpose is to spot and take shots of various typography in Singapore.
I compiled the photographs into a booklet that is placed in a handmade folio.
